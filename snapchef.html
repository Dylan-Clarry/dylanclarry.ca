SnapChef

Snapchef is a phone application for Android and iOS platforms. It finds recipes for users and allows them to create their own based on various inputs such as pictures of ingredients or text list of ingredients. In this case study I will give an overview of the roles and responsibilities that I held on throughout project.

Project Links

Teams

The project was completed as a group of 22 people for a software engineering course at Laurier. To facilitate such a large group we split up into eight teams:

Android, iOS, Backend, Database, Frontend Design, Machine Learning, Project Management, SQA

Since we allowed ourselves to be on multiple teams at once, I was on iOS, frontend design, project management, and SQA. On top of this, my responsibilities were quality assurance, version control, and documentation.

UI/UX

I was tasked with creating the apps UI/UX design. When coming up with the design I focused on making the UI and colours simple and for the UX to just focus on the main functionality of the app. All of the designs were created using Adobe Illustrator and Gravit.
